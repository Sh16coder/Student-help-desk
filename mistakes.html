Mistakes You Should Avoid ❌:

  • ❌ **Ignoring Backlogs** – Unfinished topics pile up and become impossible to manage.
  • ❌ **Focusing on Quantity Over Quality** – Solving 100 easy questions is useless if concepts aren’t clear.
  • ❌ **Skipping Note-Making** – Writing notes improves memory and helps in quick revision.
  • ❌ **Not Trusting the Process** – Constantly switching study methods wastes time and causes confusion.
  • ❌ **Ignoring Health & Sleep** – Studying late at night with less sleep affects concentration and performance.

Advice to Follow ✅:

  • ✅ **Prioritize Concept Clarity** – Focus on understanding rather than rote learning.
  • ✅ **Revise Daily** – Regular revision prevents forgetting and strengthens concepts.
  • ✅ **Stay Consistent** – Small daily efforts matter more than last-minute cramming.
  • ✅ **Solve Mixed-Level Questions** – Practice a mix of easy, moderate, and tough problems.
  • ✅ **Maintain a Balanced Routine** – Include study, breaks, exercise, and sleep for better efficiency.
